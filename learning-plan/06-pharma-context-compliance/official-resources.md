# Official Resources: Pharma Context & Compliance

## Regulatory References

### 1. FDA 21 CFR Part 11 — Electronic Records; Electronic Signatures
- **URL:** https://www.fda.gov/regulatory-information/search-fda-guidance-documents/part-11-electronic-records-electronic-signatures-scope-and-application
- **Topics covered:** Full text of the regulation, FDA guidance on interpretation
- **Relevance:** Very High — foundational regulation for pharma IT
- **Estimated time:** ~45 minutes read
- **Notes:** Focus on sections about audit trails, access controls, and electronic signatures

### 2. EU Annex 11 — Computerised Systems
- **URL:** https://www.gmp-compliance.org/guidelines/gmp-guideline/annex-11-computerised-systems
- **Topics covered:** EU GMP requirements for computerized systems, risk management, validation
- **Relevance:** High — if your company operates in or supplies to the EU
- **Estimated time:** ~20 minutes read

### 3. ALCOA+ Data Integrity Guidance
- **URL:** https://www.fda.gov/media/1199401/download
- **Topics covered:** FDA data integrity guidance for industry, ALCOA+ principles
- **Relevance:** High — understanding what "good data" means in a regulated context
- **Estimated time:** ~30 minutes read

## IT Compliance Resources

### 4. ISACA — IT Audit and Assurance
- **URL:** https://www.isaca.org/
- **Topics covered:** IT governance, audit frameworks, risk management
- **Relevance:** Medium — useful context for understanding what auditors look for
- **Estimated time:** Browse as needed

### 5. NIST Cybersecurity Framework
- **URL:** https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework
- **Topics covered:** Cybersecurity best practices, risk management
- **Relevance:** Medium — many pharma companies align with NIST
- **Estimated time:** Skim the framework overview (~20 minutes)

## Microsoft Compliance Resources

### 6. Microsoft Compliance Manager
- **URL:** https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/compliance/compliance-manager
- **Topics covered:** Compliance assessment, regulatory templates (including 21 CFR Part 11), improvement actions
- **Relevance:** High — if your org uses M365 compliance features
- **Estimated time:** ~20 minutes read

### 7. Microsoft Purview — Data Loss Prevention
- **URL:** https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/purview/dlp-learn-about-dlp
- **Topics covered:** DLP concepts, policy creation, sensitive information types
- **Relevance:** High — DLP is commonly deployed in pharma
- **Estimated time:** ~25 minutes read

### 8. Microsoft 365 Compliance Documentation
- **URL:** https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/compliance/

- **Estimated time:** Bookmark and navigate as needed

## General Guidance

### 9. GAMP 5 — Guide to Computerized System Validation
- **URL:** https://www.ispe.org/ (requires ISPE membership)
- **Topics covered:** Risk-based approach to validation, categories of systems, documentation requirements
- **Relevance:** Medium — the industry standard for validation methodology
- **Estimated time:** Reference book, not a quick read

### 10. PIC/S Guide to GMP — Chapter 4 (Documentation)
- **URL:** https://picscheme.org/docview/4186
- **Topics covered:** Good documentation practices, data integrity expectations
- **Relevance:** Medium — relevant if your company follows PIC/S guidelines
- **Estimated time:** ~15 minutes read
